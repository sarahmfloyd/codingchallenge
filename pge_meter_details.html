1. The Pilot

PG&E has developed the Pilot to provide its customers with an easy way to access and download their personalized energy usage information ("Your Data") from the My Energy page on the PG&E website (the "Site").

2. Pilot Requirements

In order to participate in the Pilot You must have (i) a My Energy Account, (ii) an electric SmartMeter™, and (iii) a connection to the Internet. In addition, You must comply with these Terms of Use and the PG&E Privacy Policy, which is located at the following URL: (http://www.pge.com/about/company/privacy/customer/).

3. How to Download Your Data

Customers can choose to share their Green Button Connect data with third parties using a secure combination of Service ID and PIN code.

- You need to have a My Energy account and an electric SmartMeter™ connected to the network.
- Login to My Energy and, from the "Pay & Manage" tab, select "Accounts and Services".
- Scroll down to find the table entitled "Services Linked to My Profile". Check the box(es) to activate Green Button Connect access to your eligible services*. Click "Add PIN" and enter a PIN code** for your selected service(s). You must accept the terms and conditions to proceed.
- You can now share your electrical consumption data with one of PG&E's participating third-party service providers.*** To grant access to your electrical consumption data via our secure API, navigate to the third-party service provider's website and provide them with both your Service ID and PIN code.
- Notes:
  * Currently, only active electrical Service IDs are eligible for Green Button Connect API access  
  ** PIN codes can be up to six numerical digits. Choose something hard for others to guess (e.g. NOT 123456).
  *** A list of links to PG&E's eligible third-party service providers is listed below, and on the My Energy Accounts and Services page

4. Backup Your Data

Once you have downloaded Your Data, You are responsible for backing up, to your own computer or other device, any important documents, images or other Your Data that you store or access via Green Button Connect. PG&E shall use reasonable skill and due care in providing Green Button Connect and access to Your Data, but PG&E does not guarantee or warrant that any Your Data you may store or access through Green Button Connect will not be subject to inadvertent damage, corruption or loss.

5. Sharing Your Data With Third Parties

One of the Pilot's primary purposes is to allow customers with a SmartMeter™ to share their personalized energy usage data with third parties. Please refer to the PG&E Privacy Policy concerning the disclosure of Your Data to third parties (http://www.pge.com/about/company/privacy/customer/).

Finally, PG&E does not warrant, guarantee, endorse, or assume any liability or responsibility to you or any other person for any third party websites, third party materials, or for any other materials, goods, or services of any third parties.
